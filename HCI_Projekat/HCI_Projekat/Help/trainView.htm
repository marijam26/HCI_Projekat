Train create, update and delete

To remove the train type from table, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'delete' shown below.


To edit the train type , first select a row in the table and then click the button 'edit' shown below.
After you click the button, a new page opens and you can edit any of the attributes. (Edit train page help).


To add new train type, click the button 'add' shown below.
After you click the button, you can add train name, train rang and other info on a new page.(Add train page help).
