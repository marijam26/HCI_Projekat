Edit train line help


On your edit train line page, you have the start, the end and the blue pin which represents an interstation. Click on one of them
and drag it on the map. Drop the pin where you want the location to be.


Then, a station dialog will pop up where you must enter the station name and the time that takes the train to arrive to the station before
and the station after (for start and end only time before). In the end click the 'save' button.


After you finish changing the train line, click on the 'save' button and confirm your action.
