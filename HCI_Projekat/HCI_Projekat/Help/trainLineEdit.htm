Edit train line help


On your edit train line page, you have start,end and blue pin which represents interstation. Click on one of them
and drag it on the map. Drop pin where you want location to be.


Then, station dialog will pop up where you must enter station name and time that takes train to arrive to station before
and station after (for start and end only time before). In the end click save button.


After you finished changing train line, click on the 'save' button and confirm your action.
