Timetable - Edit

Edit any of the fields on the page.
Edit the start and the end station by choosing the options labeled by 'from' and 'to'.


You can choose a new departure time, and a different train from the list, as shown below.

Change the start (1) and the end (2) date of the period when the added departure will be valid.

You can also change the days the timetable applies to - weekends or weekdays.
