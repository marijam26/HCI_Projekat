Choose seat help

Firstly, you have to choose wagon. You have two types of wagons: first class and second class wagon.


By choosing one of them in the square below will show its seats.


When you choose your seat if you have selected timetable with transfer place you will have to choose another seat by clicking 'next' button.
Otherwise, if you have chosen direct timetable, in the right down corner there will be 'reserve' and 'purchase' button.

By clicking 'reserve' button it will pop up confirmation dialog for reservation. You can confirm action by clicking 'confirm'.


If action is successful you will get notification.


Or you can cancel everything by clicking 'cancel' button. Purchase action has same steps.
