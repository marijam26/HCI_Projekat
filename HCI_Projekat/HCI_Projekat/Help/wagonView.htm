Wagon view

In the left upper corner you can see info about train and in the table below are shown wagon's info.





Wagon add, update and delete

If you want to add wagon to table, you have to enter wagon's info in form.
When you finish entering wagon's info,you can currently save new wagon by clicking button 'add'.


After confirming this action, new wagon will show up in the table below .


To edit wagon in table, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'edit' shown below.


After you click the button, a data will show in form and you can edit any of the attributes.

When you finish editing selected wagon's info,you can currently save them by clicking button 'save'.

After confirming this action, new wagon will show up in the table below .



To remove a wagon,you have to select a row in the table and then click the button 'delete' shown below.


To permenantely save train and wagon info you have to click 'finish' button.

Otherwise, you can cancel saving info by clicking 'cancel' button.
