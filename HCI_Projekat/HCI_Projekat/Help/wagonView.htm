Wagon add, update and delete

If you want to add a wagon, you have to enter the wagon's info in the form.
When you finish entering wagon's info, you can save the new wagon by clicking button 'add'.


After confirming this action, the new wagon will show up in the table below.


To edit a wagon in the table, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'edit' shown below.


After you click the button, the wagon data will show up in the form and you can edit any of the attributes.

When you finish editing selected wagon's info, you can currently save it by clicking the button 'save'.

After confirming this action, the new wagon will show up in the table below .



To remove a wagon, you have to select a row in the table and then click the button 'delete' shown below.


To permanently save the train and wagon info you have to click the 'finish' button.

Otherwise, you can cancel saving info by clicking the 'back' button.
