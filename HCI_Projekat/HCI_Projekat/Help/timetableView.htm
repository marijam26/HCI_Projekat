Timetable search

You can browse the timetable by choosing the parameters in the form shown below.


You can choose the line by choosing the start and the end station, labeled by 'from' and 'to' respectively.
Specify if you are searching for a weekday or a weekend departure by choosing one of the options shown below.
Search by any of the parameters, or all of them at the same time.
When you click the 'search' button, the results will be shown in the table.




Timetable create, update and delete

To remove an item from the timetable, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'delete' shown below.


To edit an item from the timetable, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'edit' shown below.
After you click the button, a new page opens and you can edit any of the attributes. (Edit timetable page help).


To add an item to the timetable, click the button 'add' shown below.
After you click the button, you can add a new departure time on a new page.(Add timetable page help).
