Report by the timetable

You can browse the timetable by choosing the parameters in the form shown below.


You can choose a line by choosing the start and the end station, labeled by 'from' and 'to' respectively.
Specify on which date you want to travel by choosing it from calendar. You also have to choose departure time. You must enter all these parameters.

When you click the 'search' button, the results will be shown in the table.
