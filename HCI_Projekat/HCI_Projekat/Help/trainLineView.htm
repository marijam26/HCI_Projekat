Train line view

You can view all train lines in table form shown below.


When you click the 'view' button, all stations of selected line will be shown in the table. There you can view and remove stations. (Train line stations page help)




Train line create, update and delete

To remove an item from the train lines, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'delete' shown below.


To edit an item from the train line, first select a row in the table and then click the button 'edit' shown below.
After you click the button, a new page opens and you can add new or change start and end station. (Edit train line page help).


To add an item to the train lines, click the button 'add' shown below.
After you click the button, you can add a new train line by draging stations to the map, on a new page.(Add train line page help).
