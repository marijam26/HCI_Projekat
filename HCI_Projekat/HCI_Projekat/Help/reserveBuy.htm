Reserve/buy ticket help

You can browse the timetable by choosing the parameters in the form shown below.


You can choose the line by choosing the start and the end station, labeled by 'from' and 'to' respectively.
Specify on which date you want to travel by choosing it from calendar.

When you click the 'search' button, the results will be shown in the table.


You can reserve or purchase a ticket for any of these timetables by choosing it from table and clicking 'choose seat' button. On the other page you can choose wagon and seat and confirm your action.(Choose seat help)

To start tutorial for this part of application you can click 'tutorial' button shown below.
