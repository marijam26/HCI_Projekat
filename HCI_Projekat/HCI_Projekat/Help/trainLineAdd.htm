Add train line help


Firstly, drag start or end pin and place it on the map where you want.


Then, station dialog will pop up where you must enter station name. Then click save button.


For the second draged pin, after name you must enter the time (in minutes) it takes for the train to arrive from start staion to end station.


After you set positions of start and end station, blue pin will show and now you can drag it to the map where you want interstation to be.
You can put as many interstation as you want but they must have different names.


For interstation you must enter two times (in minutes), time that takes train to arrive to station before and station after interstation.


After you finished making train line, click on the 'save' button and confirm your action.
