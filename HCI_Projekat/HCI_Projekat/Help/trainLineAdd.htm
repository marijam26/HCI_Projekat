Add train line help


Firstly, drag the start or the end pin and place it on the map where you want.


Then, a station dialog will pop up where you must enter the station name. Then click the save button.


For the second dragged pin, after the name you must enter the time (in minutes) it takes for the train to arrive from the start station to the end station.


After you set the positions of the start and he end station, a blue pin will show up and now you can drag it to the map where you want to add an interstation.
You can put as many interstations as you want but they must have different names.


For an interstation, you must enter two times (in minutes), the time that takes the train to arrive to the station before and the station after the interstation.


After you finish making the train line, click on the 'save' button and confirm your action.
