Manager homepage help

From the homepage, navigate to the other pages by choosing an option from the menu on the top of the page.
To view the trains , the timetables, or the train lines, click the option and then choose whether you want to view all the items, or to add to the collection.
To see the railway map with all the train lines, click the 'Railway map' and than click 'view'.
You can also see the reports on the sold tickets. Choose whether you want the report by month or by the timetable.
